Frequently asked questions

I'm not sure whether my ticket is worth fighting. Can I talk to someone over the phone?
Absolutely. If you're not ready to fight your ticket yet, call us or email your ticket to us for a free evaluation. We'll go over your options and help you decide whether it makes sense to fight the ticket.
What does your fee include?
Everything. Just one flat-fee is all you'll ever have to pay to fight your case. There are no hidden fees. We negotiate special rates with attorneys so you never pay more than you need to.
Do you fight all types of traffic tickets?
Our lawyers are traffic law experts with extensive experience fighting every type of traffic violation. Some of the most common violations we see are speeding, illegal use of the HOV lane, and negligent driving.
What outcome can I expect?
Our primary goal is to get your violation - and fine - completely dismissed. We're able to achieve this for an overwhelming majority of tickets. Sometimes, the judge won't dismiss the ticket, but reduce the violation to one that won't impact your record. We believe that no one should be penalized for contesting their traffic ticket. That's why if we fail to get the ticket off your record, you'll get a full refund, no questions asked.
